Who is Driving the Pyber Industry?



To help us understand how the ride-sharing business has revolutionized the way we travel, this data visualization aims to provide a visual navigation of the relationship between four key pyber ride-sharing variables such as average fare (in $) per city, the total number of rides per city, the total number of drivers per city and city type (urban, suburban or rural).

Included in this analysis are sets of data containing information about every active driver and historic ride, including details like city, driver count, individual fares, and city type.

So, sit back and enjoy the pyber ride.
